Employee Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many leaves do employees get per year?
A: Employees receive 20 paid leaves per year.

Q: Can unused leave be carried forward?
A: No, unused leave expires at the end of the calendar year.

Q: How many work from home days are allowed?
A: Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week with approval.

Q: Who should I contact for IT-related issues?
A: All IT issues should be reported to the internal IT support team.
