Work From Home Policy

Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with prior approval.
WFH requests must be approved by the reporting manager.

Employees are expected to be available during core working hours from
10:00 AM to 5:00 PM regardless of work location.

WFH is not permitted during critical project deadlines unless explicitly approved.
The company reserves the right to revoke WFH privileges if productivity is affected.
