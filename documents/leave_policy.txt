Company Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per calendar year.
This includes casual leave, sick leave, and personal leave.

Unused leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance through the internal portal.

Sick leave of more than 2 consecutive days requires medical documentation.
Leave approval is subject to manager discretion based on team workload.

Public holidays are defined separately and do not count toward paid leave.
